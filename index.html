FAQs

Questions? Look Here

What types of flowers do you carry?

We offer a wide variety of fresh flowers, including roses, lilies, tulips, orchids, sunflowers, and seasonal blooms. We also have plants, greenery, and specialty arrangements for all occasions.

Can I customize a bouquet or arrangement?

Absolutely! Our florists would be happy to create a custom arrangement based on your preferences.

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes, we offer local delivery and, in some cases, nationwide delivery depending on the location.

How do I care for my flowers once I receive them?

Trim stems at an angle and place in clean water with flower food. Keep away from direct sunlight, heat, and drafts.

Do you offer flowers for weddings and events?

Yes, we specialize in designing flowers for weddings, corporate events, parties, and other celebrations.

What if I need flowers for a last-minute occasion?

No problem! We offer same-day delivery for orders placed early enough.
